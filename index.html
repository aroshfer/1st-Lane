About Us

1st Lane is a vibrant Sri Lankan band known for their unique sound and energetic performances. We blend traditional Sri Lankan music with modern influences to create a captivating experience.

Formed in 2022, the band has quickly risen to fame in the local music scene, performing at numerous events and festivals across the country. Our music is a fusion of various genres, including rock, pop, and traditional Sri Lankan rhythms, which makes our sound truly unique.

Our band members are passionate musicians with diverse backgrounds, bringing their own influences and styles to the group. Together, we create music that resonates with a wide audience, from young fans to seasoned music lovers.

We believe in the power of music to bring people together and inspire positive change. Through our performances and community engagements, we aim to spread joy and promote cultural appreciation.
